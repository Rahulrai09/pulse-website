CRYOLIPOLYSIS | BODY CONTOURING | FAT REDUCTION

Cool Shape

Cool Shape uses cryolipolysis — controlled cooling — to freeze and break down stubborn fat cells without surgery, incisions, or downtime. Its 360° cooling design wraps evenly around the treatment area using medical-grade silicone cooling cups, while dual isothermal control keeps temperature precisely stable throughout. Four handpieces can run at once, each with independently adjustable cooling and suction. Frozen fat cells are gradually cleared by the body's own lymphatic system, with visible results typically appearing within 4–6 weeks and full results at 2–3 months.

A Genuine Alternative to Surgical Fat Removal

Because Cool Shape works through the body's own lymphatic clearance rather than immediate physical removal, results appear gradually and look natural rather than abrupt — closer to what clients would expect from diet and exercise than from surgery. Running four handpieces at once with independent cooling and suction control also means a clinic can treat multiple areas, such as the abdomen and flanks, within a single appointment rather than scheduling separate visits.
