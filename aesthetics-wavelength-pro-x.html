Wavelength Pro X

Wavelength Pro X combines four laser wavelengths — 755nm, 808nm, 940nm, and 1064nm — in a single platform, letting one machine safely treat hair removal across all skin tones and hair types rather than needing separate lasers for each. A large 12×28mm² spot size speeds up treatment coverage, while the C3 continuous cooling system pairs sapphire contact cooling with active cooling technology to protect the skin and keep treatments comfortable at higher energy levels. TUV SUD certification and FDA clearance back its safety credentials for clinical use.

One Machine for Every Skin Tone

Most diode lasers are optimized for a narrow band of skin tones, forcing clinics to either turn away certain clients or invest in multiple machines. By combining four wavelengths in one handpiece, Wavelength Pro X removes that limitation, letting a single system serve the full range of skin types and hair colors a clinic is likely to see. The larger spot size also means fewer passes per session, cutting appointment time without compromising on comfort or safety.
