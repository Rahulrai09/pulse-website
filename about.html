Who We Are

Transforming healthcare
through precision engineering

At Pulse, we are transforming the future of healthcare with innovation, precision, and compassion at the core of everything we do. Founded in 2025, Pulse stands as a forward-thinking medical device manufacturer committed to creating advanced, reliable, and cost-effective solutions designed to meet real clinical challenges.

Built on the vision of "India to the World," we combine India's manufacturing strength with robust engineering, quality systems, regulatory compliance, and service infrastructure to create reliable healthcare technology solutions for modern hospitals and healthcare providers.

Pulse works closely with India's MSME manufacturing ecosystem, enabling high-quality domestic manufacturing through structured R&D, standardized quality frameworks, regulatory certifications, and scalable distribution. By integrating product development, compliance, supply chain orchestration, and after-sales support under one operating model, Pulse is creating globally competitive MedTech solutions while strengthening India's healthcare manufacturing capabilities.

Our mission is clear and impactful — to enhance the quality of life through affordable and accessible medical technologies that empower healthcare professionals and improve outcomes for patients across the world.
