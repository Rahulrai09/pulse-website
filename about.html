About Pulse

Transforming the future of healthcare with innovation, precision, and compassion at the core of everything we do.

Engineering India's MedTech Future

Founded in 2025, PULSE stands as a forward-thinking medical device manufacturer committed to creating advanced, reliable, and cost-effective solutions designed to meet real clinical challenges.


As healthcare advances and patient expectations rise, we are redefining how technology supports care. By blending cutting-edge engineering with a patient-first approach, we bridge the gap between global healthcare needs and India's expanding MedTech capabilities.


Our mission is clear and impactful — to enhance the quality of life through affordable and accessible medical technologies that empower healthcare professionals and improve outcomes for patients across the world.

Our Mission

To improve quality of life by delivering world-class medical devices that combine innovation, affordability, and accessibility.

Our Vision

To be at the forefront of India's MedTech revolution, delivering accessible, affordable, and high-performance medical devices that improve patient outcomes and shape the future of healthcare.

Our Core Values

Every device we build, every decision we make, is anchored in these five principles.

  • Patient Safety First — Every device we build is engineered with the highest priority on patient well-being.
  • Innovation with Purpose — Technology is only meaningful when it solves real healthcare challenges.
  • Regulatory Excellence — We uphold global standards to ensure uncompromised safety and efficacy.
  • Sustainability — Ethical and eco-conscious manufacturing is embedded in our DNA.
  • Customer Commitment — Our relationship doesn't end at product delivery — we are partners in progress.

Our Legacy

Though young, PULSE is built on strong foundations. Established in 2025, we represent India's commitment to becoming self-reliant in healthcare technology, reducing dependency on imports, and empowering medical professionals with reliable, homegrown solutions.
