Visage

Visage combines High-Intensity Focused Electrical Stimulation (HIFES) with radiofrequency energy to lift and firm the face without surgery. The electrical stimulation strengthens the underlying facial muscles while RF energy heats deeper tissue to stimulate collagen and elastin production, smoothing fine lines as it works. Delivered through an 800W output at 1MHz RF frequency, clinical use has shown wrinkle reduction of up to 40% and a 35% improvement in muscle tone, with results building over a course of sessions.
