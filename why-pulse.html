Why Pulse Performs

Pulse is a Bangalore-based integrated medical equipment company serving pan-India hospitals and healthcare facilities. We are a single, structured partner built around the realities of medical equipment procurement — simplifying complexity without compromising control.

01 / MULTIPLE CATEGORIES

One Partner. Multiple Categories.

Managing multiple vendors across different medical equipment categories leads to procurement fatigue, inconsistent service terms, and delayed installations. Pulse consolidates this experience.

  • Single point of contact for all procurement
  • Unified billing and financing options
  • Standardized SLA across all equipment
02 / VALUE ENGINEERING

Engineered for Value

We deeply integrate with OEM manufacturers to optimize product design and sourcing. This allows us to provide equipment that hits the perfect intersection of clinical reliability and cost-efficiency.

  • Direct integration with top-tier Indian OEMs
  • Cost-optimized clinical specifications
  • No premium markups for intermediary distribution
03 / SPEED & RELIABILITY

Speed That Matters

In healthcare, delays in equipment delivery mean delayed patient care. Pulse guarantees faster demos, quicker decisions, and highly dependable delivery timelines tailored to your facility's readiness.

  • Rapid turnaround for technical demos
  • Predictable and transparent delivery schedules
  • Coordinated installation and clinical training
04 / LIFECYCLE PARTNERSHIP

Service You Can Rely On

Our commitment doesn't end when the invoice is paid. With a pan-India network of trained service engineers, we ensure ongoing uptime and rapid response for maintenance and repairs.

  • Local, on-ground service engineering teams
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling
  • Readily available spare parts inventory
